GAME OF LIFE

WebGL version

Cell calculations and rendering are performed by the GPU allowing for larger numbers of cells.

By Zombie-Einstein

Cellular automaton based on John Conway's game of life. At each turn each cell measures the state of it's neighbours and then updates it's state based on it's current state and the number of alive neighbours.
